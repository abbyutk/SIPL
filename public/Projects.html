Project Description

Badwar Maouganj Road Project Pvt Ltd proudly presents the "Rehabilitation and Upgradation of Package-11 Badwar-Tamra-Sitapur-Mauganj Road and Raipur-Karchuliyan-to Supiya-Bhaluha-Mahsaon Road to Two-Lane" project in the state of Madhya Pradesh. This transformative initiative, undertaken on a Hybrid Annuity Mode, encompasses two major road segments

This ambitious project exemplifies Badwar Maouganj Road Project Pvt Ltd's commitment to delivering high-quality, sustainable road solutions. With a construction period of 730 days for each road segment, our dedicated team is poised to implement innovative engineering practices, ensuring the timely completion of this vital transportation upgrade. We anticipate that these road developments will not only facilitate efficient travel but also contribute significantly to the overall development and prosperity of Madhya Pradesh.

Road 1 - Raipur-Karchuliyan-to Supiya-Bhaluha-Mahsaon Road:

Length of the Project: 18.656 km (from 0+000 to 18+656)

Construction Period: 730 days

This segment involves the comprehensive rehabilitation and upgrading of the Raipur-Karchuliyan-to Supiya-Bhaluha-Mahsaon Road, spanning approximately 18.656 kilometers. The project aims to enhance the road infrastructure, ensuring smoother connectivity and improved travel experiences for the communities it serves.

Road 2 - Badwar-Tamra-Sitapur-Mauganj Road:

Length of the Project: 42.777 km (from 0+000 to 42+777)

Construction Period: 730 days

The second segment focuses on the Badwar-Tamra-Sitapur-Mauganj Road, covering a substantial distance of 42.777 kilometers. Through meticulous rehabilitation and upgradation efforts, this road development initiative seeks to elevate the quality and capacity of the existing infrastructure, fostering socio-economic growth and connectivity in the region.
